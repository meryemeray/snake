GAME MODES

CLASSIC
Standard snake. Eat, grow, survive.
MIRROR
All directions are flipped!
SPEED
Gets faster with every food eaten.
SHRINK
Start huge. Eating shrinks you. Reach length 1 to win!
NO WALLS
Wrap around edges freely.
MAZE
Walls appear after each food. Don't get trapped.
POISON
Red food = poison. Loses points. 3 in a row = death.
TIMED
60 seconds. Food worth more as time runs out.
PORTAL
Two portals on the board. Step in one, exit the other.
SURVIVAL
Walls close in from the edges every 5 seconds.
GHOST
Periodically go transparent and pass through yourself.
WANDERER
Food wanders around randomly.
RUNNER
Food actively runs away from you.
REVERSE
Eating food reverses your snake.
